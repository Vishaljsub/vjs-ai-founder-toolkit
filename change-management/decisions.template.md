# Decisions Log (lightweight ADRs) — {{APP_NAME}}

Non-obvious architectural / process decisions and *why* — so future sessions don't re-litigate
them. One entry per decision, newest first. Keep it short: context → decision → why. Link related
entries and CHANGELOG commits.

Only record decisions that aren't obvious from the code. Routine choices don't belong here.

---

## YYYY-MM-DD — Short title of the decision
**Context:** What prompted this — the constraint, incident, or open question.
**Decision:** What was decided, stated plainly.
**Why:** The reasoning, including any alternatives considered and rejected.
