# Skill: Content Calendar

**Run by:** content-engine · **Output to:** `../workspace/03-content/calendar.md`

Consistency over volume. For a solo founder: **2–3 founder LinkedIn posts/week + 1 cornerstone piece/month.** Every post ties to a pillar from the messaging house.

## Weekly cadence (founder LinkedIn)
| Slot | Angle | Example |
|---|---|---|
| Post A | **Contrarian POV** | a sharp, defensible take on your category |
| Post B | **Build-in-public / lesson** | what you learned building/shipping something specific |
| Post C | **Buyer-pain story** | a real conversation that illustrates the problem |

## Monthly cornerstone
- One SEO article on a pillar keyword.
- Repurpose into 3–4 LinkedIn posts + email + sales one-pager. Write once, cut many.

## Rules
- Every piece: one idea, one CTA, one pillar. Concrete example or it doesn't ship.
- Batch-write weekly; schedule; then engage daily in comments/DMs (distribution > publishing).

### Done when
- 4 weeks planned with topic, pillar, channel, and CTA per slot; first week drafted.
