# Skill: Launch Plan

**Run by:** launch-pr-lead · **Output to:** `../workspace/06-launch/launch-brief.md`

A launch is a story with a deadline. Don't launch without ≥1 real proof point.

## Brief (fill before anything else)
- **What** we're launching (be specific — a capability, a milestone, a result).
- **Narrative** — one repeatable sentence.
- **Why now** — the reason the market should care today.
- **Proof** — the design-partner result / logo / metric. If none → delay the launch, get proof first.
- **Primary audience & channel** — default: founder LinkedIn + targeted communities.
- **Date & owner per asset.**

## Asset checklist
- [ ] Demo video / GIF (30–60s)
- [ ] Hero screenshots
- [ ] Landing page updated (content-engine)
- [ ] One-pager PDF
- [ ] 3–5 staggered social posts (tease → day-of → follow-through)
- [ ] FAQ + objection answers (competitive-intel-analyst)

## Sequence
1. **T-7:** tease.
2. **T-0:** launch post + demo + landing CTA. Reply to every comment. DM engagers → demand-gen.
3. **T+3:** reshare best reactions / early results.
4. **T+7:** retro — what created pipeline (demos, trials, replies), what to repeat.

## Product Hunt (optional)
Only with a coordinated push, a strong asset, and people ready to support day-of. Skip if it'd be a weak, unsupported launch.

### Done when
- Brief approved, assets checked off, posts drafted, metrics = pipeline (not likes).
