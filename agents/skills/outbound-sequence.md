# Skill: Outbound Sequence

**Run by:** demand-gen-strategist · **Output to:** `../workspace/02-demand-gen/sequences.md`

Problem-led, personalised, founder-sent. Goal = a discovery call, not a pitch.

## Pre-reqs
- Beachhead account list (~50) with a **specific trigger** noted per account.
- ICP personas + one-line pain each (`icp-personas`).

## 5-touch sequence (founder-led, ~12 days)
1. **Email 1 — the trigger.** Reference their specific situation. One sentence of pain, one of the product, one CTA.
2. **LinkedIn connect** (no pitch) — 1 day later.
3. **Email 2 — proof/POV.** Share the cornerstone article or a 60-sec demo. No ask beyond "does this resonate?"
4. **LinkedIn message** — short, references email.
5. **Email 3 — break-up.** "Should I close the loop?" Often the highest reply rate.

## Rules
- ≤90 words per email. No "hope this finds you well." No feature lists.
- One CTA per message. Personalise the first line for real (the trigger), not `{{firstName}}` theatre.
- Track per account: contacted → replied → call → pilot.

## Variants to draft
- One sequence per persona (economic buyer vs. champion — different pain, different CTA).
- A warm-intro version (when the founder has a mutual connection — lead with the name).

### Done when
- 2–3 sequences written, list loaded, and a scorecard exists to measure reply → call → pilot.
